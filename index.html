About Our STEM Circle

The Cedar Rapids STEM Circle is a community-driven initiative dedicated to inspiring the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators. We create engaging experiences that make STEM accessible and exciting for students from middle school through high school.

Through workshops, projects, and collaborative problem-solving, we help students develop critical thinking skills and discover real-world applications of STEM principles. Our approach emphasizes creativity, experimentation, and hands-on learning.

We welcome students of all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you're a STEM enthusiast or just beginning to explore these fields, our circle provides a supportive environment for discovery and growth.

Science

Explore biology, chemistry, physics & environmental science

Technology

Design, build, and test innovative solutions

Problem-solving and analytical thinking
