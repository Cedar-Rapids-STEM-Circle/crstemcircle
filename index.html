About Our STEM Circle

The Cedar Rapids STEM Circle inspires middle and high school students through hands-on workshops, collaborative projects, and creative problem-solving, helping them explore science, technology, engineering, and math in a supportive, engaging environment.

Science

Explore biology, chemistry, physics & environmental science

Technology

Coding, robotics, AI, and digital innovation

Engineering

Design, build, and test innovative solutions

Mathematics

Problem-solving and analytical thinking
